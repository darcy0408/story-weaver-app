# Privacy Policy - Story Weaver

**Effective Date:** November 15, 2024

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Story Weaver is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children and families who use our therapeutic storytelling platform. We believe that emotional health tools should be safe, transparent, and respectful of personal boundaries.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for parent/guardian accounts)
- Username and basic profile information
- Age range and preferences

### Usage Data
- Story creation patterns and preferences
- Therapeutic activity engagement
- Character evolution progress
- App usage statistics

### Child Safety Data
- Emotional check-ins and feelings tracking
- Therapeutic goal progress
- Coping strategy usage
- Milestone achievements

## How We Use Information

### Therapeutic Services
- Personalize emotional learning experiences
- Track therapeutic progress and growth
- Provide appropriate content based on age and needs
- Generate insights for emotional development

### Platform Improvement
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Develop new therapeutic features
- Analyze usage patterns for better support
- Ensure platform safety and effectiveness

### Communication
- Send important updates about account and safety
- Provide therapeutic progress summaries
- Share new features that may help emotional growth

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- Limited data retention policies
- Access controls and monitoring

## Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

- **With explicit parental consent** for therapeutic collaboration
- **Legal requirements** to ensure child safety
- **Emergency situations** where immediate help is needed
- **Service providers** who help operate the platform (under strict contracts)

## Children's Privacy

Story Weaver is designed for children ages 4-12. We take special care with children's data:

- **Parental Control**: Parents/guardians have full access to their child's data
- **Age-Appropriate Content**: All features designed for child development
- **No Advertising**: We do not show ads or collect data for marketing
- **Educational Focus**: All data collection serves therapeutic and educational purposes

## Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have complete control over their child's data:

- **Access**: View all data collected about their child
- **Correction**: Update or correct information
- **Deletion**: Request complete removal of data
- **Export**: Download their child's therapeutic progress
- **Consent**: Control sharing and third-party access

## Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

- **Active accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive accounts**: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Therapeutic records**: May be retained longer with parental consent for continuity of care
- **Legal requirements**: Some data may be retained to comply with laws

## International Data Transfers

If data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards:

- Standard contractual clauses
- Adequacy decisions where applicable
- Secure transmission protocols
- Compliance with international privacy laws

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have these rights:

- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of personal data
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Portability**: Receive data in a structured format
- **Objection**: Object to certain data processing

## Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking for app functionality:

- **Essential cookies**: Required for app operation
- **Analytics**: Usage patterns to improve therapeutic features
- **No advertising**: We do not use tracking for advertising purposes

## Third-Party Services

We may integrate with trusted therapeutic partners:

- **Clinical validation partners**: To ensure therapeutic effectiveness
- **Educational institutions**: For research and improvement
- **Emergency services**: For crisis resource integration

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes to this policy:

- **Email notification** for significant changes
- **In-app notifications** for policy updates
- **Grace period** to review changes before they take effect

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

- **Email**: privacy@storyweaver.app
- **Support**: In-app help section
- **Emergency**: For immediate safety concerns, contact local authorities

## Professional Disclaimer

Story Weaver provides therapeutic tools and resources, but is not a substitute for professional mental health care. Always consult qualified professionals for serious emotional or mental health concerns.

*This privacy policy is designed to be child-friendly and transparent. We believe families should understand exactly how their data is used to support emotional growth.*
